About Cat Café

Purr me coffee right meow!

The Cat Café is a coffee shop where  ten cats of varied origins live rent free in unrestricted freedom. You may build your friendship with the masters of the café while enjoying a cup of coffee, a drink and a delicious sandwich. The particularity of this "cat bar" comes in its décor, atmosphere and high quality coffee.  We wanted to create a special place in Cluj where we can share the love we have for these little felines with others.

Frequently asked questions

  • What role do Cat Café plays in the adoption process?
    In creating the Cat Café, we wanted to be a "café", but also a place where cat lovers and enthusiasts could meet and share their love for these little felines. Surprisingly many of our guests, at the end of their visit, seem to want to get a cat. We try to help them in this process, in particular in their steps of adoption, we direct them towards animal associations.
  • How do you ensure that the premises are hygienic?
    This is a matter on which Cat Café does not compromise! For this reason we have divided our space so that cats can't get in contact with the food preparation area. We clean the café several times a day with appropriate products. The ventilation has been reviewed and adapted. And also, our veterinary hygienist, Dr. Stefan, follows and validates all our processes.
  • What do you serve in your café?
    We are a coffee shop above all! Therefore, you will find a wide range of hot and cold coffees according to the seasons. To satisfy your cravings, we serve a large variety of beverages and a selection of sandwiches. All our products are fresh, that's why the menu is short! We prefer quality over quantity.
  • Can you watch my cat?
    If we answered yes to that question, there would be no room to sit! No, we are not a cat boarding facility. This is a special business that requires a special infrastructure.
